Our Goal

From the sun-kissed beaches of California to the coniferous forests of the Pacific Northwest the United States’ western seaboard has been blessed with some of the most magnificent wildlife on earth. However, the aforementioned wildlife has recently been deteriorating at a staggering pace due to man-made problems, like Climate Change. Our goal at the PWC is to unite people under the common interest in preserving the West Coast’s beautiful environment. Together we can help ensure the nature that the Pacific Coast has been given can survive for generations and continue to prosper.
